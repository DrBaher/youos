Gmail Bookmarklet

One-click email drafting from Gmail

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

📧 YouOS Draft

How to use

  1. Drag the button above to your bookmarks bar.
  2. Open any email in Gmail.
  3. Click the YouOS Draft bookmark.
  4. YouOS opens with the email pre-filled — generate a draft reply instantly.
How it works: The bookmarklet extracts the email subject, sender, and body text from the currently open Gmail thread and opens YouOS with those fields pre-filled. No data is stored until you submit feedback.
