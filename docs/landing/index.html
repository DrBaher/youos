How it works

📥

Connect your sources

Gmail, Google Docs, or WhatsApp — 5 min setup

🧠

Build your corpus

Ingests your sent history across all sources

✉️

Draft in your style

Detects intent, isolates by account, expands queries — greets by first name. Use the bookmarklet to generate and insert drafts directly in Gmail

🔄

Gets smarter overnight

Organic pairs captured, persona re-analyzed with EWMA, golden eval checks quality, rotating benchmarks prevent overfitting

Your emails never leave your Mac. The model trains locally on Apple Silicon. No cloud. No subscriptions. No tracking.

Get started

# Install YouOS
pip install youos

# Check your setup
youos doctor

# Run the setup wizard
youos setup

# Optional: ingest WhatsApp export
youos ingest --whatsapp ~/Downloads/chat.txt

# View corpus health
youos corpus
